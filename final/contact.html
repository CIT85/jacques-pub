Who Am I?

My name is Jacque, and I got the idea for my project from ChatGPT. The API help build the content as well based on prompts I created. While it gave me a few choices, I chose this project idea because during a dark time in my life, garden videos on Youtube helped me get out of my funk. I did try planting, but I lack consistency. Anyways, I really enjoyed doing this project and building the layout; however, if I had managed my time better, I would have created a cleaner and more ogranized stylesheet. Regardless, I hope you enjoy my site!
